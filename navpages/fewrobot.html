F.E.W. Robot Club

F.E.W., short for Future Engineering Workshop. Is the origin of my programming journey back in high school. I have took several robot competition including: First Tech Challenge (FTC), First Robotic Competition (FRC) and DJI RoboMaster S1 Challenge.

Although I bought my first programming book C Primer Plus in middle school and I picked up my first language from there. It was the robot that gave me the chance to do actual coding so robot can moving follow your command. Not just a program runs in a text terminal.
